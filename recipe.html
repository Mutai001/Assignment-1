Cake Recipe

Ingredients

    sugar
    flour
    eggs
    butter
    Milk

Follow these steps to bake a delicious cake:

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (177°C).
  • Grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  • Mix the wet ingredients:
    • Cream together ½ cup (113g) unsalted butter and 1 cup (200g) white sugar.
    • Add 2 large eggs and 2 tsp (9.9 mL) vanilla extract.
  • Combine the dry ingredients:
    • In a separate bowl, mix 1½ cups (188g) all-purpose flour and 1¾ tsp (8.4g) baking powder.
  • Pour the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and stir until well combined.
  • Add ½ cup (120 mL) milk and mix until smooth.
  • Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  • Bake for 30-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
